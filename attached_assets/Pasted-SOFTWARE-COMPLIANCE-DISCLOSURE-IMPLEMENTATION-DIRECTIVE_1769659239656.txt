SOFTWARE, COMPLIANCE & DISCLOSURE IMPLEMENTATION DIRECTIVES

Authoritative Build Specification — Follow Exactly

SECTION 1 — LEGAL & BRAND STRUCTURE (FOUNDATIONAL)
1.1 Entity Relationships (DO NOT CONFUSE)

NavigatorUSA is the legal operating entity.

NavigatorUSA is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Operation Fiscal Freedom is:

A program / initiative / website name

Operated by NavigatorUSA

Not a separate legal entity

All legal disclosures, privacy language, and compliance references must clearly tie back to NavigatorUSA as the operator.

1.2 Required Operator Disclosure (GLOBAL)

The following concept must appear (paraphrasing allowed, meaning must remain intact):

Operation Fiscal Freedom is an initiative operated by NavigatorUSA, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

This disclosure should appear:

In the footer

In the Privacy Policy

In the Terms of Use

SECTION 2 — NONPROFIT STATUS (ALLOWED & CONTROLLED)
2.1 What You MAY Say

You may truthfully state:

NavigatorUSA is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit

The initiative is mission-driven

The initiative works with aligned partners to support individuals

2.2 What You MAY NOT Imply

Even as a nonprofit, you must not imply:

Government authority

Benefit approval power

Guaranteed outcomes

Official determinations

Legal or financial advice

Nonprofit ≠ government ≠ regulator ≠ adjudicator.

SECTION 3 — DATA MODEL (EXPLICIT POSITION)
3.1 Data Use Statement (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

NavigatorUSA shares data. NavigatorUSA does NOT sell data.

This distinction must be consistent across:

Privacy Policy

Notice at Collection

Form disclosures

Internal documentation

3.2 Approved Sharing Language

Use only:

“Shared with affiliated or mission-aligned partners”

“Shared to respond to your request”

“Shared consistent with NavigatorUSA’s nonprofit mission”

Never use:

“Sold”

“Purchased leads”

“Lead buyers”

“Advertising resale”

SECTION 4 — FORM DESIGN & TCPA CONSENT
4.1 Consent Checkbox (MANDATORY)

Every form collecting contact info must include:

A required checkbox

Unchecked by default

Submission disabled unless checked

4.2 Approved Consent Meaning (ENGINEER MUST PRESERVE INTENT)

The consent language must clearly communicate:

By checking this box and submitting this form, I agree that NavigatorUSA, operating through the Operation Fiscal Freedom initiative, and its affiliated or mission-aligned partners may contact me by phone, text message, email, or prerecorded/artificial voice, including through automated technology, regarding my request. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.

4.3 Visibility Rules

Consent language must be:

Immediately adjacent to the submit button

Not hidden behind a link

Not bundled with unrelated terms

SECTION 5 — TRUSTEDFORM (MANDATORY IMPLEMENTATION)
5.1 Purpose

TrustedForm is required to:

Independently certify consent

Record the exact disclosure language

Produce a timestamped certificate for audits

5.2 Technical Requirements

TrustedForm must:

Load on every form

Fire after checkbox interaction

Generate a certificate URL

Never be bypassed by alternate submission paths

5.3 Required Lead Record Fields

Each submission must permanently store:

Name

Phone number

Email address

Timestamp (UTC)

IP address

User agent (if available)

Consent checkbox value

TrustedForm certificate URL

Landing page URL

Partner(s) information was shared with

SECTION 6 — RELAY / POSTING ENGINE (CONTROLLED SHARING)
6.1 Purpose

The relay engine exists to:

Share data only with approved partners

Support NavigatorUSA’s nonprofit mission

Ensure accountability and auditability

This is not a resale or auction system.

6.2 Relay Engine Rules

The system must:

Route only to pre-approved partners

Never re-broadcast data

Never modify user-submitted claims

Log all deliveries

6.3 Logging (REQUIRED)

Each partner share must log:

Partner name

Timestamp

Delivery endpoint

Success/failure status

SECTION 7 — MEMBER / AFFILIATED PARTNERS PAGE
7.1 Page Requirement

Create a public page listing:

Legal name of each affiliated or mission-aligned partner

High-level description of role

Statement of why sharing occurs

7.2 Required Disclosure Language

At the top of the page:

If you submit information through Operation Fiscal Freedom, NavigatorUSA may share your information with one or more affiliated or mission-aligned partners to help respond to your request.

7.3 Mandatory Linking

Link this page from:

All forms

Privacy Policy

Footer

SECTION 8 — PRIVACY POLICY & CALIFORNIA COMPLIANCE
8.1 Privacy Policy Must State Clearly:

NavigatorUSA is the operator

NavigatorUSA is a 501(c)(3)

What data is collected

Why it is collected

That data is shared, not sold

Who it may be shared with

How users can exercise privacy rights

8.2 California Notice at Collection (INLINE)

Near each form:

California Notice at Collection: NavigatorUSA collects the information you provide to respond to your request and may share it with affiliated or mission-aligned partners. For details on categories, purposes, and your rights, see our Privacy Policy.

8.3 “Do Not Sell or Share” Page

Even though data is not sold, include a page explaining:

NavigatorUSA does not sell personal information

How sharing works

How to submit privacy requests

SECTION 9 — COPY & UX RULES (GLOBAL)
9.1 Language Rules

Use:

“You may be eligible”

“Based on the information you provide”

“Does not guarantee outcomes”

Never use:

“See if you qualify”

“Approval”

“Guaranteed”

“Official determination”

9.2 Visual & Structural Rules

Do not:

Use government seals or insignia

Imply authority

Create urgency or scarcity

Use countdown timers

SECTION 10 — THANK-YOU PAGE REQUIREMENTS

The thank-you page must:

Confirm receipt only

State that NavigatorUSA or a partner may contact the user

Reiterate opt-out rights

Avoid timelines or promises

SECTION 11 — DATA RETENTION & AUDIT READINESS
11.1 Retention

Store all submissions and consent artifacts for minimum 5 years.

11.2 Audit Capability

For any submission, the system must be able to produce:

Consent proof

Language shown at submission

Timestamp

Partner(s) data was shared with
